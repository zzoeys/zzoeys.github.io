Hunter Biden's Laptop

Investigating the spread of information on Twitter

Get Started
Introduction

Introduction

The purpose of this project was to investigate the role of journalists and mainstream media in promoting harmful narratives. To do so, the following subquestions will be answered:

  • How did mainstream coverage change the conversation on Hunter Biden’s laptop?
  • How did mainstream coverage spread the story of Hunter Biden’s laptop?
  • Additionally, though in a much more limited manner, how did the release of the Twitter Files affect the conversation about the conspiracy theory that Twitter suppressed the New York Post’s Hunter Biden laptop article at the request of Biden’s campaign team?

The story of Hunter Biden's laptop involves a messy chain of custody of a laptop purported to belong to Hunter Biden. The contents of this laptop was first reported on by the New York Post in 2020 and was used as evidence to support the Biden-Ukraine conspiracy theory. As this was during the election, social media platforms like Twitter restricted the sharing of this article, which led to a new conspiracy theory that the Biden campaign team colluded with Twitter to censor this story. More recently in November 2022, CBS became the latest news outlet to verify that the laptop truly belonged to Hunter Biden. This story caught the eye of new Twitter CEO Elon Musk who released the Twitter Files, internal documents showing Twitter employees discussing the removal of tweets at the request of Biden's campaign team. Though this made it seem like it was true that Twitter had restricted sharing of the Post's article on Hunter Biden's laptop, the tweets requested to be removed by Biden's team were actually inappropriate images of Hunter Biden.

Methods

These above subquestions will be answered through the following:

  • Generating a word cloud with the common words in the tweets containing the term “hunter biden laptop" for each day in the chosen timeframe.
  • Visualising the relative reach of related news coverage on the topic on a timeline, then visualising the number of tweets on the topic against the news coverage of the topic.
  • Visualising the number of tweets containing the terms “hunter biden laptop”, “collusion” and “dick pics” right before and after the release of the Twitter Files.

Analysis

Analysis

Analysing the results

The role of journalists

Journalists can unintentionally promote misinformation. Even reporting the truth can ultimately sow more doubt and spread misinformation.

Elite amplification

Elon Musk gave the impression that the Twitter Files showed collusion between Biden's campaign team and Twitter to censor the New York Post story.

Confirmation bias

The initial belief that the Twitter Files showed collusion led to a confirmation bias in how people interpreted the content of the Twitter Files.

Conclusion

Ultimately, the investigation revealed how discourse on Twitter is shaped by reporting from mainstream news outlets, highlighting the importance of structural changes in journalism to tackle the spread of misinformation online.
